Idara Nusrat-e-Islamia

This is the offical website of Idara Nusrat-e-Islamia Global. Dedicted to develop and enlighten the presence of Mehdavia Ummah in todays world of technology. Few of the goals for this website is to serve as the median for connecting familes for marriage, Employers to potential employees, and share the books and videos from around the community to understand our faith better.

Survey

A survey is organised to understand the outreach of Mahdavia Community.

This survey collects information such as your current location, occupation & age amongnst other details. Such details are later used to find suitable Jobs & Marriage proposals

Your Privacy is never compromised during these services' application. strict measures are taken to protect your information. However you are given choice to enable us, to show or hide your details from the users

you can take your Survey here.

Matrimonial

Using the information collected from the survey, we intend to connect various parties searching from suitable candidate for their marriage.

Survey data is used for this program
